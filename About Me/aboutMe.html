Hobbies

The biggest hobby I have is photography. I've been taking pictures since I was 12 years old. Right now I am the official on-site photographer for my church, Heartland. I have been taking pictures for them every Sunday or major event for about 6 months now and they post the pictures I take on their website and on Facebook.

Since I am a very creative person, many of my other hobbies allow me to be as creative as I want. My main free time hobbies are:

  • Making Clay Charms
  • Metal Stamping
  • Painting
  • Hand Lettering
  • Crocheting
  • Sewing
Though I am also constantly looking for new hobbies to get into and to expand my creativeness. There is nothing I like more than finding a new hobby and learning all about it. I like hobbies that are easy for beginners to learn, but that also allow you to do more advanced techniques. The main reason is because I like having art days with my friends and family. When we are doing a hobby that is easy to learn, everyone has fun and if they really like it there is the option of learning more advanced techniques.
