Projects at Coffee Designs


Below are the projects we are currently working on. Each project will have a description of what it is, why we are making it and some additional information (ex; if someone requested we make it, what it can be used for, are there different versions of it, etc). There will also be a release date for each product.
Notice: If you are a user, you are able to purchase the new products 2 weeks before anyone else.

Also this is the first time that we are making clay products that aren't meant to be charms. These new products are supposed to be placed around your house or around rooms or even on your desk at work.



Unless Jar

Clay Unless stone
Inspiration: The Lorax

We are making this product because The Lorax is a beloved movie all throughout the company. This is something that has been in the creative stages for a while because we wanted to convey the meaning of the movie in a way that could be made big or small. So we decided to keep it simple by just having the Unless stone in a jar on top of a bed of sand.

Clay Dragon

Clay dragon holding coffee
Inspiration: How to Train Your Dragon

This was a product that has been suggested to us many times. Lots of people like giving their friends or family members figurines that are holding a coffee up or drinking coffee (for the people that like coffee). So we've decided to make a dragon that is holding a cup of coffee and this product will be fully customizable by the customer. You can make it to fit the personality of the person you are giving this to.

Heart Jar

Clay heart surrounded by clay stones
Inspiration: Pirates of the Caribbean

This project was actually suggested by some of our staff here at Coffee Designs. This is a tribute to the Pirates of the Caribbean (the first 3 movies). In Dead Mans Chest (the second Pirates movie) Jack Sparrow finds a heart and in an attempt to hide it, he puts it in a jar of dirt. Later he brags "I've got a jar of dirt and guess what's inside it." This is a favorite scene of a lot of our workers, so we decided to make a product that captured the humor of that scene as well as make a nice decoration.
