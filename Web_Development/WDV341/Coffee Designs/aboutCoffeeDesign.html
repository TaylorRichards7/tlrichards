About Us


Our goal here at Coffee Designs is to give our customers unique clay charms

This company was started because our founder realized that there were a lot of clay charms that she wanted but couldn't find anywhere that sold them. So she decided to make and sell her own. Part pf what makes these charms unique is the buyer get to customize the charms they want to buy (if they want to customize them, otherwise they can choose one of the pre-made looks).

Here at Coffee Designs we are constantly looking for new techniques to master and to bring another level of uniqueness to our product line. We also have a section on our social media pages for our customers to enter what kind of charms or techniques they would like to see us make/use
