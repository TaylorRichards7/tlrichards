Cakes and Cupcakes

Chocolate Lovers

Chocolate cake with a chocolate chip and ganache filling, white chocolate frosting, chocolate drizzle, chocolate and white chocolate shavings on top

Vanilla Bean

Vanilla cake with a buttercream filling, vanilla bean frosting, and vanilla bean shavings on top

These cookies were decorated by kids during our Kids Baking Day

Cookies

Freshly Baked Chocolate Chip Cookies

Our homemade freshly baked chocolate chip cookies are made two different ways: chewy cookie with gooey chocolate and crunchy cookie with melted chocolate

Mint Chocolate Chip

For this cookie recipe we add some peppermint to the cookie batter and we mix in some chocolate mint candies to add a more minty flavor

Sugar Cookie

We make our sugar cookies and we give them to the customer to decorate. We have a table filled with different colored frosting and lots of various toppings. We make these cookies whenever we have an event with kids - decorating the cookies is always fun for the kids


Pies were made during our Sweet and Savory class

Pies

Apple Pie

For our apple pie we use green Granny Smith apples, our homemade crust is brushed with melted butter and brown sugar, and it's always made fresh from the oven

French Silk

Our french silk pie is made in our most flaky pie crust, there are dollops of whipped cream on the top of the pie and to top it all off the pie is drizzled with chocolate ganache

S'mores Pie

This pie has a graham cracker crust, chocolate pie filling, and toasted marshmallows covering the top. Chocolate drizzle and graham cracker bits are available to put on top of the pie


These macaroons were the winners of Macaroon Day last week

Pastries

Cannoli

This delicious Italian pastry is one of our most popular items, with it's homemade crust and filling. Our cannolies come in various flavors

Eclair

This pastry dough is made at all times through out the day, when someone orders an eclair we fill them and top them where the customer can see

Macaroon

Macaroons is the staffs favorite pastry to make. These pastries allow for so many different flavor combinations. About once every two weeks we have a Macaroon Day where our staff make all different kinds of macaroons, then we give our customers free samples and have them tell us which ones they like and which ones they don't
